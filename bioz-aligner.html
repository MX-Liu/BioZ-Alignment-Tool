Quick guide

Alignment review
  1. Load one synchronized CSV such as 4_task1_synced_20hz.csv.
  2. Optionally load the matching audio file to view its waveform on the same timeline.
  3. Use the BioZ shift controls to move txt_mag3 and txt_mag4 left or right in time.
  4. Use the audio shift controls to move the audio waveform left or right to align with the signals.
  5. Click the overview plot to recenter the detail pane around the part you want to inspect.
  6. Enable segment selection and drag on the overview or detail plot to mark the interval you want to export.
  7. Save a shifted CSV if you want the BioZ shift and audio shift metadata exported with the current offset values.
  8. Save the selected segment to download the clipped CSV data and matching audio clip.
Positive shift moves BioZ later or to the right. Positive audio shift moves audio later or to the right. Negative shift moves BioZ earlier or to the left. All chart bands share the same timeline.
